# Power Systems

Batteries, voltage regulators, and current budgets. The unglamorous topic that decides whether your robot runs for an hour or browns out the instant a motor spins up.

## You can move on when you can...

- Pick a battery chemistry (LiPo, Li-ion, NiMH) and explain the trade-offs
- Size a battery from a rough current budget of your robot's parts
- Explain why motors and logic usually get separate regulated supplies
- Use a buck converter to step battery voltage down to 5V/3.3V safely
- Explain brownouts and why your microcontroller resets when a servo moves
